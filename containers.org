* Containers
** Building Images
To build images use the followin =-t= flag to pass a "tag" to the image. Use =-f= to signify the specific container.
#+begin_src bash
$ podman build -t imageName .
#+end_src

*** Fedora

  ltrace \
  binwalk \
  yara \
  p7zip \
  emacs-nox
